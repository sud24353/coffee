sourcing

  • Supplier

    • ARIALOGIX LLC decided not to invest into a manufacturing facility for the products, but rather would source them from reliable suppliers.

  • Global players

    • Product sourcing hinges on the availability of private label manufacturers in countries that have trade relationships with USA as well as plentiful availability of raw materials.

    • Mexico, Costa Rica and Peru were scouted and after evaluating pros and cons of each, Peru has been selected to produce our goods.

  • Approvals

    • Products also need be made in an FDA approved facility.

    • Our products manufactured and packed in an FDA approved facility located in Peru shall be shipped in container loads, which shall arrive in Houston port, cleared and transported to designated warehouse in Oklahoma City
